Wizards and Guilds

Welcome to the Mystic Circle, my liege. Our wizards await your command. Utilizing the runes obtained from our towers, they can cast a variety of spells to enrich our own province or inflict chaos on our enemies. They will be able to cast as many spells as we wish while they have sufficient mana and the runes required for each spell. Unfortunately, if their mana drops below 10% they will need to rest, but fear not as they will regain mana each day.

You can find a complete overview and list of spells at Mystic Overview

Self Spells

Our wizards have an impressive range of spells they can use to enhance and protect our province. Some spells will remain on our province for a period of time while others will remain in place until triggered. The duration of our self spells is based on the percentage of our acres dedicated to guilds.

Here is a brief summary of the spells available to all provinces within Utopia:

  • Minor Protection - Increases our defensive military efficiency by 5%
  • Magic Shield - Increases our defensive magic efficiency by 20%
  • Fertile Lands - Increases our food production by 25%
  • Nature's Blessing - Protects our land from Storms and Droughts for its duration. Also has a 1:3 chance per cast of instantly curing The Plague.
  • Love & Peace - Increases the birth rate of our peasants
  • Builders Boon - Decreases construction time of new buildings by 25%. Only affects buildings commenced while the spell is active.
  • Patriotism - Increases the speed at which soldiers are drafted by 30%, and provides partial protection against Propaganda.
  • Paradise - Generates a small amount of land from our exploration pool. This spell is not available during War (including End of War Ceasefire).
  • Anonymity - This spell remains until activated by conducting an attack. On our next attack we hide in the shadows as we attack our enemy. Our province name will not be displayed in the news; however, we will be penalized with reduced combat gains and no honor.
  • Shadowlight - This spell remains until activated. It guarantees identification of the province that performs the next successful thievery operation against our land.

Certain races and personalities we have chosen may also provide us with access to special spells. These exclusive spells are:

  • Greater Protection - Increases our defensive military efficiency by 5%. This spell can be cast in addition to Minor Protection.
  • Inspire Army - Inspires courage and passion within our military, reducing training time by 20% (only applies to troops trained while the spell is active) and wages by 15% for the duration of the spell.
  • Hero's Inspiration - A more powerful version of Inspire Army, reduces training time by 25% and wages by 30%. Does not stack with Inspire Army.
  • Tree of Gold - Money doesn't grow on trees, or does it? With this spell you will instantly generate a portion of your daily income
  • Invisibility - Generates a cloak of invisibility to shroud our thieves and increase their effectiveness when conducting operations by 10%. This does not affect our thieves' defensive abilities.
  • Clear Sight - Increases the vigilance of our police, providing a 25% chance to catch enemy thieves, regardless of the strength of each Thieves' Guild.
  • Fanaticism - Invokes a berserker state of mind in our military, increasing their offensive abilities by 5%, at the cost of a 3% decrease to their defensive abilities.
  • Mage's Fury - Enrages our wizards, increasing their offensive abilities by 20% while decreasing their defenses by the same amount.
  • Revelation - Increases generation rate of new scientists by 30%.
  • Town Watch - Our peasants will fight in defense of our lands. Unfortunately, as they are not trained in combat, they will also suffer heavy casualties.
  • Bloodlust - Inspires a brutal rage within our troops, increase their offensive abilities by 10% and the casualties they inflict on our enemies by 10%. However, such a bloodthirsty army will also exercise less caution ans suffer 20% more losses of our own men.
  • Aggression - Provokes our soldiers, increasing their offensive strength by 1 point while also decreasing it by 1 point.
  • Reflect Magic - Provides a protective magical barrier that provides a chance to reflect offensive spells back to the caster.
  • Quick Feet - This spell remains until activated by attacking. Decreases the time taken for our next attack by 10%.
  • Mystic Aura - This spell remains until activated. Guaranteed to repel the next offensive spell cast against you.
  • Animate Dead - This spell remains until activated. During our next defensive battle, half of our dead troops will be revived as soldiers.
  • War Spoils - We revel in our victory, and the land from any attack is immediately added to our province. Attacks conducted while War Spoils is active cannot be ambushed by our opponent.
  • Fountain of Knowledge - Increases production of science books by 10%.
  • Ghost Workers - The number of jobs required to be filled to maximise our Building Efficiency is reduced by 15%.
  • Scientific Insight Increases our science effectiveness by 5%.
  • Illuminate Shadows - Casts a watchful eye over our lands, reducing the damage caused by our enemies' thievery operations by 20%
  • Wrathful Smite - Mobilises our defensive armies, causing them to inflict higher casualties on any armies invading our lands
  • Sacred Mist - This spell lowers enemy gains by 10% on your next defensive battle
  • Hero's Inspiration - Your nobility as a War Hero inspires courage and passion with the conscripts allowing significantly faster train time and lower wages for the duration.

Support Spells

Clerics have the unique ability to support their teammates by casting certain spells on them. Only spells that are designated as support spells are able to be cast on teammates, and provinces may only cast spells to which they themselves have access. Support castable spells are:

  • Minor Protection
  • Greater Protection
  • Fertile Lands
  • Magic Shield
  • Nature's Blessing
  • Love and Peace
  • Builder's Boon
  • Inspire Army
  • Patriotism
  • Hero's Inspiration
  • Scientific Insight
  • Illuminate Shadows
  • Wrathful Smite

Sorcery

Our wizards can also assist in attacking and weakening our enemies. They can cast spells that will cause damage over several days and others that will cause immediate damage. Due to the aggressive nature of some spells, they may only be cast on our enemies that have a more combative relationship to our kingdom. The success of our offensive spells depends on how many wizards we have per acre (modified by factors such as Honour, racial or personality bonuses, spells, and science), the enemy's wizards per acre (modified by the same factors), and is also affected by the relative networth of our provinces. The duration of fading offensive spells we cast is affected by our guild percentage. The damage caused by instant offensive spells we cast can be increased if we invest in Cunning science, and will be decreased if our enemy has invested in Shielding science.

The spells that may be cast by any province against our enemies include:

  • Crystal Ball - Knowing more about your friends and opponents is vital to your success as a leader. This spell gives you insight into the workings of any province of your choice.
  • Storms - Invoke the power of mother nature to kill a small portion of our enemy's peasants each day. Cancels any Droughts currently afflicting the target.
  • Droughts - Call upon mother nature to decrease our enemy's food production by 25%, military draft rate by 15%, and horse production by 50% each day. Clears any Storms currently ravaging the target.
  • Greed - Encourages enemy's military to demand 25% additional wages while active.
  • Explosions - Mines the trade routes of the province with explosives that have a chance to destroy a portion of any aid sent to or from the target province.

The exclusive spells available to certain races or personalities include:

  • Gluttony - Persuade the enemy population to consume 25% more food.
  • Vermin - Causes enemy food stockpiles to decay, at the rate of 6% per day.
  • Pitfalls - Place magical traps on target province that increases military casualties by 25%.

The aggressive spells that require an unfriendly relationship between our kingdom and the target's kingdom include:

  • Fireball - Calls down a large fireball to destroy a portion of the enemy's peasants.
  • Lightning Strike - Direct lightning towards the enemy's towers to destroy a portion of the enemy's rune stockpiles.
  • Mystic Vortex - Summons a mystical vortex that has a chance to remove each active spell on the target province.
  • Tornadoes - Destroys a small portion of the enemy's buildings.
  • Land Lust - An expensive and difficult spell that removes our enemy's land and awards it to us.
  • Abolish Ritual - Reduces the strength of an enemy kingdoms ritual by 2%. Limited to 10 casts on a single enemy province

The specialized spells that certain races or personalities can cast against the provinces of an unfriendly kingdom are:

  • Expose Thieves - Illuminates the enemy's lands to reduce the available stealth of their thieves.
  • Fool's Gold - Casts a reverse alchemy spell on the enemy to turn a portion of their gold into useless lead
  • Nightmares - Disrupts the sleep of a portion of the military, causing troops to be returned to our training queue, while basic soldiers will simply quit the army and return to our peasantry.
  • Meteor Showers - A devastating spell that will kill a portion of peasants and troops at home each day for several days.
  • Chastity - Convince the enemy's population to stop reproducing and cease all peasant births.
  • Blizzard - A short duration spell that will reduce building efficiency by 10%
  • Amnesia (War Only) - Reduces enemy science effectiveness for the duration of war.
